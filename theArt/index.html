WHAT
IS CALLED ART?

Art, also called (to distinguish it from other Art forms) visual art, a visual object or experience consiously created through an expression of skil or imagination.

7 TYPES OF ART

It can be created through painting, drawing, sculpture, music, dance, and many other forms. Art allows individuals to communicate ideas and feelings without using words. It also reflects culture, history, and the imagination of the artist. Through art, people can inspire others and see the world from different perspectives.
